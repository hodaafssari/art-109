Hoda Afssari | Digital Media Art Student

Hoda Afssari, is originally from Seattle, Washington, currently pursuing a BFA in Digital Media Art and a minor in Psychology at San Jose State University. Her diverse artwork explores themes ranging from societal issues to personal emotions. Hoda believes in pushing creative boundaries and harnessing the power of art for positive change. In her professional roles. Hoda possesses a versatile skill set encompassing Adobe software, digital media, and marketing, which she has leveraged in her professional roles as Deputy Area Manager for Helping Hand For Relief and Development, Hoda's art invites viewers to engage in open-minded discussions, and her commitment to exploring new mediums reflects her dedication to positive change through creativity. Explore her work and accomplishments on her artist website. Statement: In my artistic journey, my dedication to pushing boundaries and embracing challenges is evident. I channel my current mood to fulfill my diverse artistic vision, reflecting my identity as both an artist and an individual. From shedding light on societal issues, like the misrepresentation of Muslims in media, to exploring personal emotions and life experiences, my creations address a variety of themes, showcasing the depth and breadth of my artistic identity. I firmly believe in the power of art as a humanitarian tool, aiming to make a positive impact on the world through my creative expressions. As viewers engage with my work, I hope they approach it with curiosity, an open mind, and a willingness to participate in discussions. The boldness and vibrancy of my creations encourage viewers to take a closer look. Throughout my work, there's a recurring theme inspired by my own struggles with identity and culture—the overarching belief in finding silver linings and utilizing one's tools. Hoda Afssari's art is, above all, a reflection of my multifaceted identity, my eagerness to explore new mediums, and my commitment to making a positive impact on the world through creativity.
